Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

1. Data Collection & Privacy

We take your privacy very seriously. In BlueKeyboard:

  • Local Encrypted Storage: Your master password and all Bluetooth connection data are encrypted locally on your device using SQLCipher.
  • No Data Upload: The app does not have any remote data upload functionality, so we cannot access any of your personal privacy data.

2. Permission Request Explanation

  • Bluetooth: Required permission. Used to search and connect Bluetooth HID devices (simulating physical keyboard).
  • Biometrics: Optional permission. Used only to unlock the app locally, we will not touch your fingerprint or facial recognition raw information.

3. Disclaimer of Liability

The Bluetooth HID simulation feature provided by the app is affected by hardware compatibility. Users should bear potential data input risks caused by connection interruptions or other force majeure factors during use.

Important Reminder: Due to end-to-end encryption, if you lose your master password, this software cannot assist you in recovering the encrypted data.
